# Sugar_Cosmetics_Instagram_Analysis
### Power BI Dashboard project for Instagram Analysis.
Sugar Cosmetics is a popular Indian beauty brand founded in 2015 by Vineeta Singh and Kaushik Mukherjee, known for its cruelty-free makeup and skincare products tailored for Indian skin tones and textures. The brand operates as a direct-to-consumer (D2C) and omnichannel business, selling products through its website, online marketplaces, and a growing number of physical stores. 

This project analyses the Sugar Cosmetics Instagram handle performance, trends in content performance, audience demograhics and growth trends. Thus helping achieve data driven insights to refine and support social media strategy.
